DADDY'S FAVORITE! - Hybrid animation music video

DADDY’S FAVORITE! is a 2D/3D hybrid animated short inspired by the song of the same name by REYSHA RAMI.

The film explores themes of power, desire, and self-expression through a queer, feminist lens — reclaiming narratives of sexuality and agency through bold, expressive animation.
